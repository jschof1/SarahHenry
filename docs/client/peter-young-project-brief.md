# Peter Young - Website Redesign Brief

## Project Overview

Peter Young is a professional celebrant offering wedding and ceremony services. The current website does not meet the original project brief and requires significant updates to align with the client's vision and brand identity.

---

Peter has a background in the design industry, which means he has a keen eye for visual details and expects a high standard of design work. He emphasises that visual presentation is crucial because clients need to be drawn in by imagery before they will focus on text.

  ## Design Direction

- Celebrant sites should generally favour personality over sterile convention.

- Louder, more characterful design is often preferred, even if it is less restrained than a standard corporate site.

- The aim is not AAA-level purity at the expense of visual identity.

- Make sure text remains readable, but do not flatten the design into something generic.

- Section backgrounds should be bold and attention-grabbing — use loud, vibrant colours rather than safe neutrals. Default to high-contrast palettes and incorporate background images or graphics wherever possible. Bland or low-contrast sections are a sign the design needs more energy.

## Motion Recommendations

- Use animation thoughtfully and visibly.
- Framer Motion and GSAP are preferred tools.
- Fancy entrance animations are encouraged.
- Keep native browser scroll behaviour.
- Avoid enabling smooth-scroll systems that can feel like lag to non-technical clients.
